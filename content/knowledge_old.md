# Course: Public Speaking for Students

## Short Summary

The Public Speaking course helps students become more confident, clear, and engaging speakers. Students learn how to manage fear, use body language and voice effectively, organize ideas, connect with an audience, and practise a final speech.

## Who This Course Is For

This course is suitable for beginners, including students who feel nervous, shy, or afraid of speaking in public.

No previous experience is needed. The only requirement is a willingness to learn and practise.

## Age Group

Suitable for students of any age.

## Format

This course is available in both individual or group sessions

## Duration

8 sessions
Each session is 90 minutes

## Course Outcomes

By the end of the course, students should be able to:

- Speak with more confidence
- Manage public speaking anxiety
- Use body language more effectively
- Improve voice control, pace, and tone
- Structure ideas clearly
- Use storytelling to make speeches more memorable
- Connect better with an audience
- Deliver a practised final speech

## Course Modules

1. Introduction
   Students learn what makes a great public speaker and how to evaluate their current speaking skills.

2. Developing Confidence
   Students learn how to recognize and manage public speaking anxiety, build confidence before and during a speech, and use positive self-talk.

3. Body Language and Non-Verbal Communication
   Students learn how posture, gestures, eye contact, and movement can support a strong speech.

4. Voice Control and Vocal Variety
   Students practise pitch, volume, pace, tone, and inflection to keep the audience engaged.

5. Making Ideas Compelling and Memorable
   Students learn how to use storytelling, examples, and clear structure to make their message more interesting.

6. Understanding the Audience
   Students learn why audience understanding matters and how to build a connection with listeners.

7. Speech Preparation
   Students learn how to organize, structure, rehearse, and prepare an effective speech.

8. Final Practice
   Students practise their final speech, receive feedback, and improve before presenting.

## Price

The Public Speaking course fee is AED 1,200.

The fee includes all 8 sessions and classroom materials.

Payment plans or discounts must be handled by a human advisor.

## Common Questions

Question: Does my child need previous experience?
Answer: No. The course is designed for beginners and is suitable for students who are nervous about public speaking.

Question: Will this help a shy child?
Answer: Yes. The course includes confidence-building, anxiety management, positive self-talk, and final speech practice.

Question: Is the course online or in person?
Answer: The Public Speaking course is currently offered in person at Dubai Knowledge Park.

Question: Do students get a certificate?
Answer: Students receive a certificate of completion after attending the course and completing the final practice session.

Question: Can parents attend the class?
Answer: Parents do not usually attend the classroom sessions, but they may speak to the programme advisor about progress updates.
